## 🎯 What is Solatro?

**Solatro** is a solo adaptation of the famous poker roguelike game, designed to be played with just a standard deck of 54 cards (including 2 Jokers). 

No opponents. No pressure. Just you, your deck, and your poker skills.

Built entirely in HTML5 + JavaScript — **zero dependencies, works offline, runs anywhere**.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎴 **Classic Poker Hands** — Royal Flush, Straight Flush, Four of a Kind, Full House, Flush, Straight, Three of a Kind, Two Pair, Pair, High Card
- 🃏 **Joker System** — Bank jokers and use them as wildcards (but be careful — drawing a second one while holding one makes you lose both!)
- 🛍️ **Bonus Shop** — Spend excess points on powerful abilities: Extra Card, Extra Discard, Pocket Points, Extra Hand, Double Score, Shield
- 🌍 **8 Languages** — English, Español, Français, Deutsch, Italiano, Português, 中文, Русский
- 📱 **Fully Responsive** — Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- 🎨 **Balatro-Inspired Design** — Neon aesthetics and retro pixel font

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Draw 8 cards** into your hand
2. **Make poker hands** (up to 5 cards) to score points
3. **You have 3 plays** and **3 discards** per round
4. **Reach the target score** to advance to the next round
5. **Spend excess points** in the Bonus Shop for powerful abilities
6. **Survive as long as you can!**

### Joker Rules
- Jokers are **automatically banked** when drawn
- A banked Joker can be used as a **wildcard**
- If you already have 1 Joker and draw another → **you lose both!**
